---
title: "Meetings (Zoom, Meet, Teams)"
description: "Add Keyframe personas to video meetings"
---

The [Recall AI](https://recall.ai/) integration allows you to add Keyframe personas to video meetings on **Zoom**, **Google Meet**, and **Microsoft Teams**. The persona joins the meeting as a participant with a camera feed, and meeting audio is piped to the avatar for real-time conversation.

Note that personas added to meetings leverage Keyframe's STT\+LLM\+TTS in addition to video. You'll be able to customize the LLM, TTS and more when you dispatch a persona to a meeting.

## Prerequisites

- [Sign up](https://docs.keyframelabs.com/platform.keyframelabs.com) to the Keyframe Labs platform and [create a new API key](https://docs.keyframelabs.com/guides/getting-started/get-your-api-key)
- A [Recall AI](https://recall.ai/) API key

## Adding a Keyframe persona to a meeting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a meeting in Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams">
    ![Image](/images/image-3.png)

    You'll need the meeting url for the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and dispatch a Keyframe persona to the newly created meeting">
    ```shellscript
    curl -X POST "https://api.keyframelabs.com/v1/meet-bot" \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer $KEYFRAME_API_KEY" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d "{
        \"meeting_url\": \"https://meet.google.com/xyz-abcd-efg\",
        \"persona_slug\": \"atlas_persona-1.5-live\",
        \"recall_api_key\": \"$RECALL_API_KEY\",
        \"system_prompt\": \"You are a friendly meeting assistant.\",
        \"greeting\": \"Hey everyone!\"
    }"
    ```

    You can see the full list of configurable parameters [here](https://docs.keyframelabs.com/api-reference/meet-bots).
  </Step>
