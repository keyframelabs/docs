## Summary

Congratulations on your first call with a Keyframe persona. A couple things worth highlighting:

- You exchanged your Keyframe API key for an ephemeral Keyframe session token
- Similarly, you generated ephemeral credentials for your LLM or agent of choice
- The Keyframe Elements SDK uses these ephemeral credentials to establish all necessary real-time audio/video connections to display and interact with your persona
